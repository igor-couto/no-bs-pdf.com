    Private by design — your files never leave your device

    Most “online PDF editors” upload your document to a server you have to trust. This one doesn’t. Your PDF is read with the browser’s File API, edited in memory, and saved back out with the browser’s own download — it is never transmitted anywhere. There is no backend, no account, no cookies, no localStorage and no analytics. It even works with your network disconnected once you load the page.
